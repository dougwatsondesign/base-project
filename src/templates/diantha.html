Problem

Diantha was a brand designed for an Argentinian startup in women's apparel. They needed a brand that would convey elegance and appeal to high-end markets.

Challenges

As a high-end clothing manufacturer, their logo needed to be a work of art in itself, and needed to maintain flexibility across a variety of applications. They also needed a name that reflected the elegance of the customers they served.

Solution

The name chosen was "Diantha", which was Greek for "flower" and represented beauty and grace. To match the beauty of this name, I created a logo that hinted at mirrored, cursive D's while simultaneously creating the abstract shape of a flower in bloom. Lastly the shape in the middle resembled both a diamond and a flower stem, accomplishing multiple layers of messaging and bringing the logo together gracefully. The logo also lent itself wonderfully to bags, boxes, clothing tags, and more.
